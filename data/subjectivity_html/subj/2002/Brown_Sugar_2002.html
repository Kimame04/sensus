CONSENSUS
Though you can guess the outcome of Brown Sugar at the beginning, Lathan and Diggs imbue this romantic comedy with a lot of charm.

CAST & CREW
Taye Diggs, Sanaa Lathan, Mos Def
Directed by Rick Famuyiwa
more...

SYNOPSIS
Rick Famuyiwa breathes a refreshing burst of air into the romantic comedy genre with BROWN SUGAR, a film that is as much a tribute to hip-hop as it is a celebration of friendship and love. more...
