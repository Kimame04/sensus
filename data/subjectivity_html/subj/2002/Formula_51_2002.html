CONSENSUS
Filled with profanities, Formula 51 is a stylized and incoherent mess that doesn't add up to much.

CAST & CREW

SYNOPSIS
Formula 51 is the story of Elmo McElroy (Samuel L. Jackson), a streetwise American master chemist who heads to England to sell his special new formula -- a powerful blue concoction guaranteed to take you to the 51st State. more...
