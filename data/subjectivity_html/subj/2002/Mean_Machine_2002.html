Mean Machine (2002)


MEAN MACHINE
OVERVIEW
CONSENSUS
Despite some genuine wit, this crowd pleaser is filled with too many cliches.

CAST & CREW
Vinnie Jones, David Kelly, David Hemmings
Directed by Barry Skolnick
more...

SYNOPSIS
In this remake of THE LONGEST YARD, Robert Aldrich's 1974 Burt Reynolds prison tale concerning inmates who organize themselves into a football team and compete against the guards, British director Barry Skolnick pulls together a ruthlessly funny cast and changes the resident sport to soccer. more...

MPAA RATING
R, for language and some violence

RUNTIME
1 hour, 39 minutes

RELEASE DATES
Theatrical: Feb 22, 2002
Video: Jul 16, 2002

RELEASE COMPANY
Paramount Classics

GENRE
Comedies, British, Prison/Prisoners, Sports, Remake
