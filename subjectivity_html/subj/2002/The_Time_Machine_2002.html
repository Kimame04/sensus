CONSENSUS
This Machine has all the razzle-dazzles of modern special effects, but the movie takes a turn for the worst when it switches from a story about lost love to a confusing action-thriller.

CAST & CREW
Guy Pearce, Jeremy Irons, Orlando Jones
Directed by Simon Wells

SYNOPSIS
Scientist and inventor Alexander Hartdegen is determined to prove that time travel is possible. His determination is turned to desperation by a personal tragedy that now drives him to want to change the past. more...
