WHO IS CLETIS TOUT?

NUMBERS

CONSENSUS
Cliched and endlessly self-referential, Who Is Cletis Tout? doesn't add anything original to the crime genre.

CAST & CREW
Christian Slater, Richard Dreyfuss, Tim Allen
Directed by Chris Ver Wiel
more...

SYNOPSIS
While in prison, Trevor Finch (Christian Slater), an ex-convict specializing in identification falsification, meets a magician and jewel thief, Micah (Richard Dreyfuss). Together, they bust out and take on new identities, planning to dig up a pile of diamonds that Micah buried under a tree. more...

MPAA RATING
R, for language, some violence and sexuality

RUNTIME
1 hour, 32 minutes
